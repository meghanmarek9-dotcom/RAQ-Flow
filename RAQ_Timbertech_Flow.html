📐 How to Prepare for Your Consultation

A little prep goes a long way. Here's what to have ready before your contractor calls.

📏
Know your space dimensions
Rough measurements of your project area help contractors give you faster, more accurate quotes.
🎨
Gather style inspiration
Save photos of decks or finishes you love to share during your consultation — or browse TimberTech.com first.
💵
Have a budget range in mind
Even a rough range helps your contractor tailor their recommendations and materials to what works for you.
🏗️
Check HOA or permit requirements
Some areas require permits or HOA approval before construction. Your contractor can help guide you through the process.
